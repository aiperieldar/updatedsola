What is Sola?

Sola is an application that will allow parents to keep track of their child school progression and feel more involved in their child's life

It keeps parents on top of the school calendar and help them participate in events or volunteer opportunities.

It’s really easy to keep parents informed about student's attendance and check their daily homework.

It sends real-time communication home, including examples of student’s behavior and work.
